Our Story

From Dubai to the World's Oceans

Aroos Al Mohit — Bride of the Ocean — was founded on a singular conviction: that the maritime world's most complex logistics challenges deserve a partner who combines deep market knowledge with genuine personal commitment.

Based in the heart of Deira, Dubai — one of the world's great maritime crossroads — we operate at the intersection of shipowners and cargo interests. Every fixture we handle is matched with precision, every negotiation conducted with integrity, and every client relationship built on trust.

Our reach extends across the Arabian Gulf, Indian Ocean, Red Sea, East African coast, and beyond. We don't just broker ships — we engineer solutions that move commerce forward.
