Our Story

Who We Are

Aroos al Mohit was founded on a conviction that the maritime world's most complex logistics challenges deserve a partner who brings both deep market knowledge and genuine personal commitment to every transaction. In a world of algorithms and automated platforms, we believe that the best fixtures are still negotiated by people who know the market, know the owners, and know their clients.

Our name — Aroos al Mohit, the Bride of the Ocean — reflects our relationship with the sea: one of reverence, mastery and belonging. The ocean has connected civilisations for millennia, and it remains the lifeblood of global trade. We are privileged to operate at the heart of it.

From our base in the Arabian Gulf, we serve cargo owners, commodity traders, industrial companies and project developers across the Middle East, South Asia and East Africa. Our network of shipowners spans every major flag state and vessel category.

We don't chase volume. We pursue excellence — on every fixture, for every client, every time.
