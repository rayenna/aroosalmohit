Our Services

End-to-End Maritime Solutions

Ship Chartering

We act as your professional intermediary between cargo interests and shipowners — sourcing the right vessel, negotiating charter parties, and managing the fixture from brief to completion. Whether it's a voyage charter, time charter or COA, we deliver with speed and precision.

Our Process

From Enquiry to Fixture in Six Steps

A clear, disciplined process that keeps you informed and in control at every stage.

01
Cargo Brief

You share your cargo details, loading window, ports and requirements. We ask the right questions upfront.

02
Market Search

We canvas our owner network and the open market simultaneously — privately and discreetly — to identify suitable tonnage.

03
Owner Negotiations

We negotiate directly with shipowners on your behalf — freight rates, laycan, terms — until we have the best offer on the table.

04
Charter Party

We finalise the charter party or booking confirmation, reviewing every clause to protect your interests.

05
Fixture Recap

You receive a clean, comprehensive recap of all agreed terms — your single source of truth.

06
Voyage Support

Our involvement doesn't end at fixture. We remain available for post-fixture queries, laytime disputes and voyage updates throughout.
