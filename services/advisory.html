Our Services

Five Areas of Expert Advisory

Each advisory service is tailored to your specific commercial situation — no generic advice, just relevant, actionable expertise.

Freight Market Analysis

Regular written market commentary on dry bulk, tanker and general cargo freight markets — Baltic index movements, regional supply-demand dynamics, seasonal trends and forward outlook. Tailored to your trading desk's specific commodity and route exposures.

Voyage Estimation & TCE Analysis

We build detailed voyage estimates and time-charter equivalent (TCE) calculations for any proposed fixture — helping you understand the true commercial value of any freight or charter proposal before you commit.

Charter Party Review

Independent review of charter party documents — proforma and negotiated versions — identifying unfavourable clauses, hidden risks and areas for improvement. Particularly valuable for companies new to chartering or reviewing non-standard forms.

Laytime & Demurrage Management

We assist cargo owners in accurately calculating laytime, monitoring statement of facts, preparing or defending demurrage claims, and negotiating demurrage settlements with shipowners — reducing leakage on every voyage.

Shipping Procurement Strategy

For companies with regular cargo programmes, we advise on optimal procurement strategy: spot vs. COA, voyage vs. time charter, risk allocation, and freight budgeting — building a shipping programme that delivers consistency and cost control.
