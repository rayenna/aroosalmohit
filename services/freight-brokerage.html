Our Role

Freight Solutions Without the Vessel Commitment

Not every cargo movement requires a full vessel charter. For smaller shipments, complex multi-port itineraries, or situations where market rates are volatile, freight brokerage provides access to competitive space on existing services — without taking on vessel risk.

Aroos Al Mohit's freight brokerage service gives you access to our vetted network of shipowners, operators, and liner services across the Middle East, Indian Ocean, and global markets — with real-time rate intelligence to ensure you always pay a fair price.

We handle everything from initial rate enquiry to booking confirmation, bill of lading coordination, and post-shipment support.

Cargo Coverage

Cargo Types We Handle

From standard dry bulk to out-of-gauge project cargo — our freight network covers every commodity type moving through the Middle East and Indian Ocean.

Dry Bulk

Grain, coal, iron ore, bauxite, fertilizers, sugar, salt, and other dry bulk commodities in bulk vessel quantities and parcel sizes.

Break Bulk & Project Cargo

Steel products, timber, bagged cargo, palletised goods, and oversized project cargo requiring special stowage and handling arrangements.

Liquid Bulk

Vegetable oils, chemicals, methanol, molasses, and other liquid bulk commodities requiring tank vessels with appropriate certifications.

Containerised Cargo

Full container loads (FCL) and less-than-container loads (LCL) on liner services across the Middle East, South Asia, and East Africa.

Heavy Lift & OOG

Out-of-gauge and heavy lift cargo — transformers, industrial equipment, modular units, and construction machinery requiring specialised vessels.

Multipurpose

Mixed cargo consignments requiring flexible stowage, multi-port itineraries, or cargo combinations that don't fit neatly into a single vessel type.

Our Process

How Freight Brokerage Works

A simple, transparent process from enquiry to cargo delivery.

01
Cargo Enquiry

Submit your cargo details — type, quantity, origin, destination, and required shipment window.

02
Rate Sourcing

We approach our network of owners and operators for competitive freight quotes on your specific cargo and route.

03
Rate Negotiation

We negotiate on your behalf, securing the best available rate and booking terms — including demurrage and laytime provisions.

04
Booking & Support

We confirm the booking, coordinate documentation, and provide ongoing voyage support through to delivery.
