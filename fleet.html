Vessel Categories

Our Vessel Expertise

Six vessel categories. Every commercial trade. Deep expertise in each.

Dry Cargo
Bulk Carriers

The workhorses of global dry cargo trade. We broker bulk carriers across every size category, from small Handysize vessels suited to congested ports and minor bulk trades, to giant Capesize ships moving iron ore and coal at scale.

  • Handysize (25,000–40,000 DWT) — Bagged goods, minor bulks, fertilizers, regional trade
  • Handymax / Supramax / Ultramax (40,000–65,000 DWT) — Grain, fertilizers, coal, agri-products
  • Panamax / Post-Panamax (65,000–100,000 DWT) — Coal, grain, phosphate, bulk trade
  • Capesize (100,000+ DWT) — Iron ore, coal, bauxite, large bulk trades
Commodities Grain, coal, iron ore, fertilizers, cement, clinker, bauxite, gypsum, sugar, salt
Liquid Cargo
Product & Chemical Tankers

Clean and dirty product tankers for the transportation of refined petroleum products, vegetable oils, and IMO-classified chemicals. Active in the Arabian Gulf, Indian Ocean and East African product tanker markets.

  • MR Tankers (25,000–55,000 DWT) — Clean petroleum products, vegetable oils, chemicals
  • LR1 / LR2 (55,000–110,000 DWT) — Naphtha, jet fuel, gasoil, fuel oil
  • Chemical Tankers (5,000–40,000 DWT) — IMO II & III chemicals, specialty liquids
Commodities Gasoil, jet fuel, naphtha, palm oil, sunflower oil, molasses, chemicals
Crude Oil
Crude Oil Tankers

For the movement of crude oil from producing regions to refineries worldwide. We broker across Aframax, Suezmax and VLCC segments for clients with crude oil and fuel oil requirements.

  • Aframax (80,000–120,000 DWT)
  • Suezmax (120,000–200,000 DWT)
  • VLCC (200,000–320,000 DWT)
Trades Arabian Gulf exports, Indian Ocean crude flows, East African crude
General Cargo
General Cargo & MPP Vessels

Multipurpose and general cargo vessels are the most versatile workhorses in the fleet — capable of carrying breakbulk, bagged goods, steel, timber, machinery, and project cargo in a single hold configuration.

  • Tween-deck and single-deck configurations
  • Onboard cranes (typically 25–500 MT SWL)
  • Box-shaped holds for maximum stowage efficiency
  • Heavy lift capability on specialist units
Commodities Steel products, timber, bagged rice/cement, machinery, vehicles, project cargo
Container
Container Feeders

Regional container feeder vessels connecting secondary ports to mainline services — or providing direct port-to-port container services across the Gulf, Indian Ocean and East Africa.

  • Small Feeders 300–1,000 TEU — Intra-Gulf, short-sea services
  • Regional Feeders 1,000–3,000 TEU — Gulf–India, Gulf–East Africa
Services Intra-Gulf, Gulf–India, Gulf–East Africa, Gulf–Red Sea
Offshore
Offshore Support Vessels

Platform supply vessels (PSV), anchor handling tug supply vessels (AHTS), and crew boats supporting the oil and gas sector across the Arabian Gulf and beyond.

  • PSV — Platform Supply Vessels
  • AHTS — Anchor Handling Tug Supply
  • Crew Boats — Fast crew transfer
  • Work Barges, Accommodation Vessels, Construction Support
Operations Oil & gas field supply, construction, offshore installation support
